Challenge

The challenge of the lab was to create pseduocode and understand how pseudocode is helpful to better understand problems.

Problems

The problems I ran into were some of the steps with the instructions. I wasn't really sure what the pre code stuff was and what we were supposed to do but after messing around with it for a bit it looked correct.

Reflection

The assignment was simple and easy to understand. It was a little tedious to write down every step but it is necessary to understand the steps of coding. I have some experience with coding so I knew about the concept of pseudocode so I did not run into any issues with writing the pseudocode
